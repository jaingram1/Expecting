Website Overview

This website is made for expecting parents that helps them before the baby arrives. The website consists of different options like suggested doctors and hospitals, helpful tips from other parents, and food places that offer healthier selections for pregnant women. From these choices, expecting parents can schedule appointments or set reminders from confirmed appoinments. Another tool that it suggests is the food page. The food page shows different restaurants that pregnant woman can eat and choose different healthy choices. There are many more pages like resources where it shows helpful tips. These essential tips are advice from other parents that have gone through the same experiences.
